Welcome to GCC Website

Who we are?

Grace Community Center is an apostolic church that is committed to the application of the gospel of the Kingdom of God in the lives of the people. It is our deep desire to seek more understanding of the word of God under the guidance of the Holy Spirit because all we know is not all there is to know. God is revealing more truth from His word to equip and prepare the Church for the second coming of Christ.

Our community?

GCC is are part of a Global Network of autonomous Churches referred to as Kingdom Community Network which is one of the sectors of Congress WBN
